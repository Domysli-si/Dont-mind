# User Manual

## Getting Started

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Enter your email address and click "Prihlasit se pomoci magic linku"
3. Check your email and click the magic link to log in
4. You will be redirected to the dashboard

## Installing the App

dont-worry is a Progressive Web App and can be installed on your device:

- **Chrome/Edge (Desktop)**: Click the install icon in the address bar
- **Chrome (Android)**: Tap the "Add to Home Screen" banner or use the browser menu
- **Firefox**: Use the "Install" option in the browser menu

## Mood Tracking

1. Navigate to "Nalada" from the sidebar or bottom navigation
2. Select your mood on the 1-10 scale
3. Optionally add a note about how you feel
4. Click "Ulozit" to save

## Journal

1. Navigate to "Denik"
2. Write your thoughts in the text area
3. Click "Ulozit zaznam" to save
4. The app automatically analyzes the sentiment of your entry (positive, neutral, or negative)

## Analytics

Navigate to "Analytika" to view:

- **Nalada v case** -- Your mood trend over the last 14 days
- **Rozlozeni nalady** -- Distribution of your mood values
- **Trend sentimentu** -- How your journal sentiment changes over time
- **Korelace nalady a sentimentu** -- Relationship between mood and journal sentiment

## Settings

Navigate to "Nastaveni" to configure:

- **Motiv** -- Choose between light, dark, or system theme
- **Upozorneni** -- Enable or disable daily mood reminders
- **Uvodni pruvodce** -- Show or hide the onboarding guide

## Offline Mode

The app works offline. Any mood entries or journal entries created while offline are stored locally and automatically synced when your connection is restored. A yellow "Offline rezim" label appears when you are offline.

## Recommendations

The app provides personalized recommendations based on your mood patterns and journal sentiment. Visit the dashboard to see suggestions tailored to your well-being.
